Ink Gouache

Activated Charcoal
Calcium Carbonate
Rice Vinegar
Vodka Or Rubbing Alcohol
Gum Arabic
Honey

Mix pigment and calcium carbonate, then slowly add to an emulsion of rice vinegar, and vodka. Add a teaspoon of gum arabic to a teaspoon honey.

Let ferment in a cool cabinet for up to four weeks before using.

Ink Gouache Oil Stick

Activated Charcoal
Calcium Carbonate
Rice Vinegar
Linseed Oil
Vodka Or Rubbing Alcohol
Gum Tragacanth or Methyl Cellulose
Honey

Mix pigment and calcium carbonate, then slowly add to an emulsion of rice vinegar, linseed oil, and vodka. Add a teaspoon of gum tragacabtg to a teaspoon honey.

Mold into sticks, and allow to air dry for three to five dags.

[ Optional ] You can make the ink first, and use that as the pigment.
